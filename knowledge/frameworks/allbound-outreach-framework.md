# Allbound Outreach Framework

## Allbound vs Traditional Approach

| Traditional Pipeline | 2025 Allbound Pipeline |
|---------------------|----------------------|
| Generic content lacking trust | Trust-building content before outreach |
| Disconnected sales and marketing | Unified sales and marketing strategy |
| Cold outreach with low engagement | AI-powered, scalable systems |
| Inefficient, manual lead sourcing | Signal-based + evergreen outreach |
| Low-quality leads that don't convert | Leveraging referrals and past leads |

## Demand Generation Activities

- **LinkedIn ICP Expansion**: Send 20-40 connection requests daily to target buyer personas (no selling, just connect and build visibility)
- **LinkedIn Content**: Publish 2-3 high-value posts per week to warm up network
- **Webinars**: Run monthly webinars or quarterly summits with relevant topics
- **Paid Ads**: Create ads focused on building brand awareness and nurturing future buyers
- **Cold Outreach**: Share valuable resources or event invites (not pushing to book calls)

## LinkedIn Best Practices

### Do's
- Send empty connection requests
- Send low-key messages
- Optimize for high connection rate
- Target people who: Posted on LinkedIn, Have 500+ connections, Share common connections

### Don'ts
- Pitch in first interaction
- Send pitch five days after connecting
- Max out LinkedIn connection limitations

## Content Strategy (Iceberg Approach)

| Funnel Stage | Percentage | Content Type |
|--------------|------------|--------------|
| TOFU (Top) | 50% | Thought leadership, industry trends, insights, personal stories |
| MOFU (Middle) | 30% | Buyer education, common challenges, misconceptions, guidance |
| BOFU (Bottom) | 20% | Conversion content, case studies, social proof, product impact |

### Posting Structure
- TOFU: 6 posts/month
- MOFU: 4 posts/month
- BOFU: 4 posts/month

## Cold Outreach Rules

- Never run cold outbound from primary domain
- Create separate Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 account to avoid spam flags
- Treat cold email/LinkedIn as demand generation channels (share valuable resources)

## Referral Strategy

### Right People
- Prioritize relationships over potential
- Focus on those who know your ICP: customers, past deals, advisors, partners, industry peers

### Right Timing
- Leverage key moments: onboarding, key milestones, renewal

### Right Ask
- Be specific: "Who do you know in X role at Y company facing challenge Z?"
- Make it easy: Provide pre-written email or LinkedIn message
- Leverage reciprocity: Offer insights, discounts, or recognition
- Follow up and track in CRM

### Right Culture
- Incentivize: Reward successful referrals
- Reinforce: Celebrate and recognize referrers
