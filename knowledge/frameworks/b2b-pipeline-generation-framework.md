# B2B Pipeline Generation Framework

## Four Ways to Grow Revenue

- **Pipeline Generation** - Building pipeline with qualified prospects
  - Metric: Lead Velocity Rate (month-over-month qualified pipeline growth)
- **Pipeline Conversion** - Turning conversations into closed deals
  - Metric: Sales Velocity (speed of opportunities to revenue)
- **Customer Base Growth** - Getting existing customers to buy more
  - Metric: Net Revenue Retention
- **Sales Efficiency** - Doing more with less through smarter processes
  - Metric: Revenue generated per dollar spent

## Bowtie Model (Modern Sales Pipeline)

- Revenue flows **before and after** purchase (unlike traditional funnel)
- **Left Side** - New prospects moving through awareness, education, selection to win
- **Right Side** - Existing customers growing, referring, changing jobs creates new opportunities

### Pipeline Sources from Existing Customers

- Upselling and cross-selling
- Expansion to new departments/divisions
- Price increases
- Referrals from happy customers
- Champion/buyer job changes
- Partner ecosystems
- Closed-lost resurrection (competitor contracts ending, blockers removed)

## In-Market vs Out-Market Truth

- On any given day, only ~5% of TAM is actually ready to buy ("in-market")
- 95% are out-market because:
  - Current setup works fine
  - Never heard of solution
  - Just signed with someone else
  - Don't see this as priority
- Focus on prospects showing intent signals and those warmed through demand generation

## Key Principles

- Build recognition before asking for time
- Pay attention to signals of interest
- Respect when prospects aren't ready
- Focus on those showing mutual interest
- Nurture relationships for long-term
- Cold outbound works but conversion rates improve significantly when prospects know your brand first
