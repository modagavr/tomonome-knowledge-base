# Intent Signals Framework

## Three Types of Intent Data

| Type | Source | Reliability |
|------|--------|-------------|
| First-Party | Direct interactions with your assets/channels | Most reliable |
| Second-Party | Trusted partnerships | Broader context, maintained quality |
| Third-Party | Market-wide signals | Broadest view, early-stage opportunities |

## Signal Types and Strength

### High Strength (★★★)
- Booked a call and discovery quiz completed
- Compliance updates (new regulations driving demand)

### Medium-High Strength (★★)
- Pricing page activity (frequent visits with interaction)
- Monitor job changes (champion changes company/department)
- Monitor promotion (champion promoted)
- New hires (monitor new hires of ICP)
- Market expansion (firms entering new markets/geographies)
- Customer wins or losses
- Industry disruptions
- Technology stack changes

### Medium Strength (★)
- Content engagement (newsletter opens, webinar signups, blog reads, video watches)
- Competitor engagement (LinkedIn profiles engaging with competitors)
- Industry thought leader audience
- Search intent data (ICP accounts searching specific keywords)
- Mergers and acquisitions
- Funding rounds
- Product launches
- Competitor developments
- Major partnerships
- Review websites (G2, Trustpilot insights)
- GitHub data (repository watchers/contributors)
- Online communities

## Signal Processing Framework

### 1. Noise - Filter Out Low-Quality Signals
- Filter casual website visits, low-engagement email opens, accidental ad clicks
- Filter non-ICP accounts and non-relevant buyer personas
- Use AI and automation to filter

### 2. Nurture - Educate and Build Engagement
- For signals showing early interest but not ready for direct sales
- Use email workflows, offers, lead magnets, retargeting ads, educational content
- Warm them up until they show stronger intent

### 3. Convert - Act on High-Intent Signals
- For signals indicating strong buying intent (demo requests, pricing page visits, deep content engagement)
- Funnel directly into sales or personalized outreach for timely follow-up

## Signal Capture Tools

- **LinkedIn Engagement**: Trigify.io (monitors LinkedIn interactions)
- **Website Visitors**: Syftdata.com (identifies and deanonymizes website visitors)
- **Data Enrichment**: Clay.com (qualifies, scores, enriches, personalizes messages)
- **CRM**: HubSpot (integrates with GTM tools)
