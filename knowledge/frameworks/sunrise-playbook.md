# Sunrise Playbook (Inverted Idea Stage)

## Core Concept
- Start with **demand** (buyer priorities) before defining **supply** (product)
- Avoid traditional "idea maze" by securing market need before building
- Minimizes risk of building something nobody wants

## Key Principles

### Deal Velocity Power Law
- Solutions addressing buyer's **#1 Priority**: close in weeks
- Solutions addressing lower priorities (#2+): take 12–18 months
- Fast deals → fast feedback → fast iteration → capital attraction

### Risk Reduction
- Build "case study factory" with validated demand
- Ensure product-market fit before significant build effort

## Framework Steps

### 1. Find Demand (Pre-Idea)
- Engage target buyers (C-level) **before** forming product hypothesis
- Identify **Pull**: buyer priorities where existing solutions are insufficient
- Goal: discover urgent pain points buyers will pay to solve

### 2. Nail Supply (Solution Design)
- Define product shape based strictly on identified demand
- Present solution sketches/concepts to same buyers

### 3. Secure Validation (Early Commitments)
- Obtain early customer commitments/contracts based on concept
- Use commitments as validation that demand is real

### 4. Build and Scale
- Build product to fulfill specific commitments
- Establish repeatable case studies across referenceable customers
- Launch publicly once repeatable model is proven

## Prerequisites & Constraints

### Deal Size
- Most effective for enterprise sales with **$50k+ ACV**

### Access Requirements
- Need established networks to access C-level buyers
- Leverage investors/advisors for high-volume, high-quality conversations

### Focus Constraint
- Must target buyer's **top priority only**
- Solving secondary problems → slow sales cycles
