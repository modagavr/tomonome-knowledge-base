# Ideal Client Profile Reframe

## Core Concept
- ICP is not who you want to sell to, but who would be **weird not to buy** upon hearing about your solution
- Causality is inverse: **the ICP chooses the startup**, not vice versa
- Focus on finding existing demand where your solution is the obvious choice

## ICP Definition Criteria
- Target customers who convert **~75%+ of the time**
- Customers who are **95%+ satisfied post-sale**
- No pushing or persuasion required

## Two Required Factors
A valid ICP must include both:

1. **A certain kind of person** - specific persona characteristics
2. **A certain kind of situation**:
   - They have a specific project on their to-do list
   - They currently face bad options for solving it

## Application
- Stop "boiling the ocean" with broad targeting
- If you need to push sales, add features, hire more salespeople, or renovate your sales process → you have a **demand-side issue**, not a supply or skills issue
- Focus on identifying prospects where your solution is the natural, obvious choice
